Biography

Jessica Randall is a student from East Lansing, Michigan. She studied Media Arts and Technology at Michigan State University, and is drawn to website design and development. She's passionate about designing the look, layout and organization of websites, as well as graphically designing the content. During undergraduate studies at MSU, Jessica worked in freelance design where she gained skills and knowledge to better her technique in her areas of interest. Jessica has been recognized for the artistic qualities and level of professionalism she brings to her work. In addition to design and development, Jessica is an active part of physical training. In her spare time, you'll find Jessica choreographing in the dance studio or running as many miles as she can. Jessica lives by the motto, "Never be afraid to trust an unknown future, to a known God." Jessica's personal slogan, "Make the lasting impression of love," reminds her that family and faith will consistently be her guide.
